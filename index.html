Who We Are

About 4 SONS Security

Founded in 2018, 4 SONS Security is a professional security training centre dedicated to developing disciplined, highly skilled security personnel. We provide certified security guard training and reliable guarding services for private and public premises across South Africa.

Our mission is simple: produce security professionals who carry themselves with confidence, professionalism, and integrity, officers who protect people, property, and peace of mind.
